Components list for ESP32 SENDER:

1. ESP32 DevKit V1 - 1 piece
2. Microphone amplifier module GY-MAX4466 - 1 piece
3. MB102 3.3V/5V breadboard power supply module - 3 pieces (for stock)
4. 9V battery + battery clip with 2.1x5.5mm jack plug - 1 piece
5. Standard breadboard - 1 piece
6. Push buttons (momentary switch) - 2 pieces
7. 10 kΩ resistors (for pull-up, if not using INPUT_PULLUP in the code) - 2 pieces
8. Male-male jumper cables for breadboard connections - at least 20 pieces
9. (Optional) 9V 1A power supply with 2.1x5.5 mm jack plug - 1 piece (alternative to 9V battery)
